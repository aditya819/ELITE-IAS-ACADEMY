FAQ's

It is usually not required to travel to Delhi for UPSC preparation because most of the reputable coaching schools have many locations. Apart from this, online classes and study materials are always available to assist with preparation from anywhere in the country. In fact, online courses are preferred by working professionals to weekend batch.

Students may be less inclined to stick to their study routine if they receive online mentoring. Offline coaching, on the other hand, will not allow pupils to lose motivation because they will be physically present in class and will be required to adhere to a strict timetable. Though many people find online coaching beneficial because of the numerous benefits, you will need a good internet connection to participate. If you live in a location where internet access is poor or non-existent, coaching centres are your only option for studying for the UPSC exams.

Individuals who do not have a bachelor’s degree from a recognised university are not eligible to take the IAS exam. However, one might enrol in a foundation course at a coaching centre to prepare for the upcoming IAS exam. Choosing coaching after the 12th will help you achieve your objective of becoming an IAS officer.

A graduate degree from a recognized university is required of the candidate. Age Limit for UPSC Civil Services Exam: Candidates must be at least 21 years old and no older than 32 years old. EWS & General Category: 32 years; 6 tries. A graduate of a recognized university is required of the candidate. To become an IAS officer, no minimum percentage is required. An IAS officer can be a graduate from any field.

Elite IAS Academy allows students to register online for admission, making it easier and faster for them. Following each registration, a token number is generated, and candidates are admitted based on the number. It should be noted that candidates who register early have a larger selection of batches to choose from. As a result, getting enrolled as soon as possible is highly recommended!

When you have to cover a syllabus as extensive as the UPSCs, every minute counts. Enrolling in a coaching institute can eliminate that extra work from your routine, allowing you to focus solely on your ultimate goal.
